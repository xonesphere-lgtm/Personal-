A Letter To My
Future Wife

❤

My dearest,

A year ago, I said yes to a future I could not yet see. I trusted Allah's plan, even without knowing what the path ahead would look like.

Today, I am grateful for this journey of patience and waiting. Not for what has already happened, but for everything that is still to come, InshaAllah.

I look forward to the simple moments — sharing life after long days, standing beside each other in ease and difficulty, and building a home filled with sakoon, mercy, and barakah.

I do not know everything about you yet, but I look forward to learning you — your laughter, your silence, your dreams.

This year of waiting has been sabr, and I pray what comes next is filled with khair, ease, and Allah's blessings. Ameen.

Until then — I remain here. With patience. With hope. And with sincerity.

With all my heart

— Jazil
